Before You Go

Intelligence to Gather Pre-Event

Six data points that would sharpen this model and your event strategy significantly.

🗂️
HubSpot Contact Audit
Which of the 27 ICP districts already have contacts in HubSpot? Warm outreach vs. cold changes your meeting conversion rate by 2–3x. Pull the list before you pack.
🎯
Attendee Role Mix
CAMT skews teacher-heavy. If only 15% of attendees are district-level buyers (not 25%), your meeting count drops by 40%. Confirm the audience profile with the event organizer.
📐
Standards Alignment Status
CO is flagged as not fully aligned in the targeting doc. Know which grade bands and standards are coverage gaps — it's the first objection you'll face with serious buyers.
🏆
Cherry Creek Reference Playbook
You have a win here. Build a 2-minute reference story before the event: who's the champion, what measurable result did they see, and will they take a reference call?
🔍
Competitive Presence at Event
Check the exhibitor list. If DeltaMath, IXL, or ALEKS are on the floor, your qualification conversation needs to address the comparison directly. Know their talking points cold.
📅
District Fiscal Year Timing
Most CO districts run July–June fiscal years. A June event means budget decisions are typically made or already locked for FY26. Position conversations toward FY27 pipeline, not immediate close.
